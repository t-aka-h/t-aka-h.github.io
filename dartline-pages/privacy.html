Dartline — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-17 (v1.1.0 — initial release)

Dartline ("the App") is developed and operated by an independent developer. This Privacy Policy describes how the App handles user information.

Data Collection

The App itself does not collect personal data on developer servers. Local game state — best Count Up score, settings — is stored on your device using iOS UserDefaults.

The App displays advertisements via Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect device identifiers (e.g., IDFA when granted via App Tracking Transparency, or anonymous identifiers when not granted) to serve and attribute ads. Please see "Advertising (AdMob)" below for details.

Motion Sensors

Dartline uses the iPhone's accelerometer and gyroscope (via the CoreMotion framework) to detect tilt for aiming and throw motion for releasing darts. Motion data is processed entirely on-device in real time and is never transmitted off the device or stored persistently.

Advertising (AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to display interstitial ads (full-screen ads shown between game sessions, after the result screen at a low frequency with cooldown to avoid back-to-back display).

To respect Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework, the App will ask for your permission before tracking activity across other companies' apps and websites. You can decline this prompt and the App will function exactly the same — only ads will be less personalized.

Google AdMob's privacy practices, including details on data collection and EU/US user rights (GDPR/CCPA), are documented at: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

The App enforces a maximum content rating of "Parental Guidance" (PG) at the SDK level, blocking sexual content, dating, gambling, alcohol/tobacco promotion, and similar categories from appearing as ads. We additionally block sensitive ad categories on the AdMob console.

Game Center

If you choose to sign in to Game Center (a service provided by Apple), the App submits your Count Up best score to Apple's leaderboard. Score submission is handled entirely by Apple's GameKit framework. The developer does not have access to your Game Center identity or scores beyond what is publicly visible on the leaderboard.

Game Center is governed by Apple's privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Local Storage

The App stores progress data (Count Up best score, theme preferences) locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

Third Parties

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — interstitial advertising. PG-rated content only. See "Advertising" section above.
  • Apple's own frameworks (SwiftUI, CoreMotion, Core Haptics, GameKit, AVFoundation, App Tracking Transparency) — these are not third parties.

Children

The App is suitable for ages 12 and up (Apple Age Rating 12+). It does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or the equivalent age in your jurisdiction).
