FOLDLINE

RHYTHM × ORIGAMI PUZZLE

Foldline is a 60-second folding puzzle where matching colors collapse and the rhythm grows with your chain. Long-press and swipe to fold the board in 8 directions; drag to shuffle tiles before they fall. Every chain syncs to the beat.

How to Play

  • Fold — long-press and swipe in any of 8 directions to fold the board. Matching colors cancel out when they overlap.
  • Shuffle — drag with one finger along a path to slide tiles into alignment.
  • Time — you have 60 seconds. Chains of 5+ award bonus time, and the BPM accelerates as time runs low.
  • Mini-games — chains of 10+ trigger one of six bonus mini-games. A PERFECT run grants +15 seconds.
