Foldline — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-05 (v1.0.0 — initial release)

Foldline ("the App") is developed and operated by an independent developer. This Privacy Policy describes how the App handles user information.

Data Collection

The App itself does not collect personal data on developer servers. Local game state — best score, total play time, settings — is stored on your device using iOS UserDefaults.

The App displays advertisements via Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect device identifiers (e.g., IDFA when granted via App Tracking Transparency, or anonymous identifiers when not granted) to serve and attribute ads. Please see "Advertising (AdMob)" below for details.

Advertising (AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to display interstitial ads (full-screen ads shown between game sessions, after the game-over screen at a low frequency with cooldown to avoid back-to-back display).

To respect Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework, the App will ask for your permission before tracking activity across other companies' apps and websites. You can decline this prompt and the App will function exactly the same — only ads will be less personalized.

Google AdMob's privacy practices, including details on data collection and EU/US user rights (GDPR/CCPA), are documented at: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

The App enforces a maximum content rating of "Parental Guidance" (PG) at the SDK level, blocking sexual content, dating, gambling, alcohol/tobacco promotion, and similar categories from appearing as ads. We additionally block sensitive ad categories on the AdMob console.

Game Center

If you choose to sign in to Game Center (a service provided by Apple), the App submits your best score and total play time to Apple's leaderboards. Score submission is handled entirely by Apple's GameKit framework. The developer does not have access to your Game Center identity or scores beyond what is publicly visible on the leaderboard.

Game Center is governed by Apple's privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Local Storage

The App stores progress data (best score, cumulative play time, BGM preference, onboarding completion flag) locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

Third Parties

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — interstitial advertising. PG-rated content only. See "Advertising" section above.
  • Apple's own frameworks (SwiftUI, Core Haptics, GameKit, AVFoundation, App Tracking Transparency) — these are not third parties.

Children

The App is suitable for ages 4 and up (Apple Age Rating 4+). It does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or the equivalent age in your jurisdiction).
